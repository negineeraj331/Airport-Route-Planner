How It Works

Graph Representation

Cities are nodes in the graph. Direct flight routes are edges connecting these nodes, weighted by distance.

Dijkstra's Algorithm

Finds the shortest path between two nodes by systematically exploring nearest nodes first.

Optimal Routes

Computes the most efficient route by minimizing total distance traveled between origin and destination.
